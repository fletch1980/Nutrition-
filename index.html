🧠 Mind-Muscle Cues

Hip Thrust
Drive through your heels. Squeeze glutes for 2 full seconds at the top. Feel it in the glute, not your lower back.
Cable / Band Kickback
Slow down. Squeeze at full extension for 1–2 sec. If you feel it in your hip flexor or back, you've gone too fast.
Romanian Deadlift
Feel the hamstring stretch on the way down. Drive hips forward to stand — glutes finish the movement.
Sumo Squat
Push knees out. Pause at the bottom. Squeeze inner thighs and glutes together on the way up.
Every Exercise
If you don't feel the target muscle, drop weight 20% and slow down. Mind-muscle beats heavy and sloppy every time.
